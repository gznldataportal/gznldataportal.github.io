Comprehensive Data Integration

Each dataset in our portal is meticulously annotated with developmental stage or disease condition metadata, enabling researchers to perform cross-sectional analyses, track molecular changes across development, and identify disease-specific signatures. Our multi-omics approach (Genomics, Transcriptomics, scRNA-seq, scATAC-seq, Proteomics, and Metabolomics) provides unprecedented resolution for understanding lung biology.
